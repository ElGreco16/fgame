# Privacy Policy for Vasilias tis Salougkas

**Effective date:** July 1, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **AntonoGames** ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Android game **Vasilias tis Salougkas**, also identified in some development materials as **Football Heads** (the "Game").

## Information we collect

The Game does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or payment information.

The Game may collect the following limited information:

- **Anonymous identifier:** the Game creates and stores an identifier used to distinguish one installation or player from another. It is not intended to identify you by name. The identifier contains random values and a shortened cryptographic hash derived from a device identifier.
- **Gameplay and app activity:** selected character IDs, opponent IDs, AI type and difficulty, match mode, match result and score, input method, whether golden goal occurred, and similar gameplay events.
- **Settings activity:** changes to touch-control mode, button size, control layout, collision mode, and keyboard bindings.
- **Technical information processed by our service provider:** when analytics are transmitted, our service provider may automatically process network and request information such as IP address, timestamps, and basic device or connection information needed to operate and secure its service.

The Game also stores settings, tournament progress, and the anonymous identifier locally on your device.

## How we use information

We use this information only to:

- understand how the Game is played;
- diagnose bugs and performance problems;
- balance gameplay, AI, controls, tournaments, and powerups;
- remember local settings and tournament progress;
- protect the analytics service from misuse; and
- improve the Game.

We do not use this information for targeted advertising, credit decisions, or tracking you across other companies' apps or websites.

## Sharing and service providers

We use **Talo**, a game-services provider, to identify anonymous players and process gameplay analytics. When analytics networking is enabled and available, the anonymous identifier and gameplay or settings events described above may be transmitted to Talo over an encrypted connection.

Talo processes this information on our behalf to provide player identification and event analytics. We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

Information may also be disclosed if reasonably necessary to comply with law, enforce our rights, protect users or the public, investigate fraud or security issues, or complete a business transfer subject to appropriate safeguards.

## Data retention and deletion

Local settings, tournament progress, and the anonymous identifier remain on your device until you clear the Game's storage or uninstall the Game. Android backup is disabled for the Game.

Analytics information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law. You may request deletion of analytics information associated with your anonymous identifier by contacting us. Because the Game does not collect your name or email address, we may need information from your installation to locate the relevant anonymous record.

To request access, correction, or deletion, contact **georgantonopouloss@gmail.com**. We will respond as required by applicable law.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures designed to protect information. Analytics are transmitted using encrypted HTTPS or secure WebSocket connections. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's privacy

The Game is not intended to collect personal information from children. It does not ask players to submit names, contact details, location, photos, or other user-generated personal content. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Game, contact us at **georgantonopouloss@gmail.com** so we can investigate and delete it where appropriate.

If the Game is listed on Google Play as directed primarily to children, we will review all included SDKs and data practices for compliance with Google Play Families requirements before publication.

## International processing

Our service providers may process information in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

## Your rights

Contact us to exercise a right. These rights may be subject to legal limitations.

We may update this Privacy Policy when the Game, its data practices, or legal requirements change. We will update the date above and publish the revised policy at the same public location. Material changes will be communicated as required by law.
